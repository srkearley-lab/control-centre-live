Demo Website Brief

Local listing is doing too much of the sales work; a dedicated premium barber site could convert searches into bookings.

Brief

Build a self-contained premium homepage concept for De'Milia Barber that feels credible enough to show to a local business owner as a £1,000 website proposal.

Design Direction

sharp, confident, premium, traditional barber craft with a modern booking journey.

Audience

men looking for a sharp Brentwood barber, walk-ins, regular trims, skin fades, beard shaping, and tidy local booking.

Required Homepage Sections

  • Premium hero with local positioning and clear CTA.
  • Services, example pricing, why choose us, trust signals, testimonials marked as placeholders, and contact CTA.
  • Local SEO wording for Brentwood/Shenfield searches.
  • Responsive, self-contained HTML and CSS.

Acceptance Criteria

  • Looks polished and commercial.
  • Does not look like a wireframe.
  • Includes pricing with the confirmation disclaimer.
  • Uses premium CSS visual panels if no AI images are available.
  • Demo homepage is over 15,000 bytes.
