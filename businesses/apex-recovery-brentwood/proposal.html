A focused website upgrade plan for Apex Recovery Brentwood

Premium sports recovery and mobility clinic in Brentwood helping gym-goers, runners and active adults reduce stiffness, recover faster and move better.

Executive summary

Apex Recovery Brentwood has a clear opportunity to present its local offer with more authority, stronger visual direction, and a simpler enquiry journey.

The proposed direction is a premium, mobile-first website demo that helps visitors understand who the business helps, what the service experience feels like, and what to do next. No existing website supplied.

Current situation

Current website status: No website

Based on the submitted prospect details, Apex Recovery Brentwood would benefit from a clearer online presence that explains the offer quickly, builds local trust, and gives visitors an easy next step.

Notes: Create a premium sales-ready website demo with strong visuals, local conversion focus, service sections, trust-building copy, polished proposal, and outreach email that could be sent to the business owner. Do not include prices.

Opportunity

Clearer positioning

Visitors need to understand who the business helps, where it operates, and why it is a good local choice within a few seconds.

Better enquiry path

A stronger enquiry route can reduce friction and help interested customers take action without hunting for contact details.

Local search relevance

Local service and location copy can give the business a better foundation for organic discovery over time.

Recommended improvements

  • Premium homepage structure with a strong hero, service messaging, trust signals, and direct calls to action.
  • Local SEO copy aligned with the business description and Brentwood-area search intent.
  • Clear visual hierarchy so customers can scan the offer quickly on mobile and desktop.
  • Visual sections that feel specific to the business category, not like placeholder content.
  • Safer redesign handling when an existing website is supplied, using limited context rather than copying content.

Recommended package

Premium Local Website Demo Package

A polished one-page website concept, local positioning copy, call-to-action structure, responsive layout, lightweight SEO foundation, proposal support page, and manual outreach draft.

Business value: a stronger first impression and a clearer route from visitor interest to enquiry.

This does not guarantee search rankings, leads, sales, or revenue. It creates a stronger base that can be reviewed, tested, and improved.

Deliverables

  • Sales-ready website demo tailored to the submitted business description.
  • Responsive structure for mobile and desktop review.
  • Service, trust, local SEO, visual direction, and enquiry sections.
  • Proposal page to support the sales conversation.
  • Concise outreach email draft for manual review only.

Timeline

Step 1

Review the demo for accuracy, offer fit, and tone.

Step 2

Confirm services, locations, proof points, imagery approach, and preferred enquiry action.

Step 3

Turn the approved direction into a refined live-ready version.

Optional add-ons

  • Additional service pages for high-intent local searches.
  • Google Business Profile copy refresh and photo guidance.
  • Review capture prompts and testimonial placement.
  • Conversion tracking plan for calls, forms, and important clicks.

Risks of doing nothing

  • Potential customers may compare against competitors with clearer, more trustworthy websites.
  • Mobile visitors may leave if services, location, and next steps are not obvious.
  • Local search pages may stay too thin to support long-term organic discovery.
  • Outreach has less impact if the demo does not feel polished enough to start a serious conversation.
