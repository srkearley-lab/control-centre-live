# Outreach Compliance Rules

## Purpose
Keep Shane's website prospecting workflow professional, safe, and compliant.

## Rules

1. Do not send emails automatically.
2. Shane must approve every outreach message before it is sent.
3. Prefer public business-facing email addresses such as info@, hello@, enquiries@, contact@.
4. Avoid personal/private email addresses unless there is a clear business context.
5. Do not spam businesses.
6. Do not scrape aggressively.
7. Only use publicly available business information.
8. Do not make false claims about a business, its website, its SEO, or its rankings.
9. If a business says no, stop, unsubscribe, remove me, or not interested, mark it as Do Not Contact.
10. Do not contact the same business repeatedly without a sensible reason.
11. Always keep outreach short, respectful, and non-pushy.
12. Demo websites must be clearly described as examples or concepts.

## Required Opt-Out Line

If this is not relevant, just reply "no thanks" and I will not contact you again.

## Approval Rule

No outbound message is sent unless Shane explicitly approves it.
