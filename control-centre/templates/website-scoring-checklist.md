# Website Scoring Checklist

Use this checklist to assess whether a local business is a good website prospect.

## Scoring

- No website: +3
- Website looks outdated: +2
- Poor mobile experience: +2
- No clear call to action: +1
- Poor branding/design: +1
- Business likely depends on local search: +1
- Clear contact details available: +1
- Strong visual/demo opportunity: +1

## Priority

- 8–10: High priority
- 5–7: Medium priority
- 1–4: Low priority

## Review Notes

For each business, check:

- Does the website load properly?
- Does it look modern?
- Is it mobile-friendly?
- Is there a clear contact button?
- Is the service offering obvious?
- Is the branding strong?
- Could a simple one-page website make it look much better?
