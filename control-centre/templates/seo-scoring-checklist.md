# SEO Scoring Checklist

Use this checklist to identify businesses with weak local SEO or poor website visibility.

## Basic SEO Checks

- Page title exists and is relevant
- Meta description exists
- Homepage clearly states service and location
- Main heading explains what the business does
- Contact details are easy to find
- Opening hours are visible where relevant
- Google Business Profile appears to exist
- Website appears in local search results
- Website uses HTTPS
- Website loads reasonably quickly
- Website is mobile-friendly
- Pages have clear service descriptions
- Images have useful names or alt text where visible
- No obvious broken pages
- No obvious outdated copyright/date
- No excessive popups or confusing layout

## SEO Score

1-3: Poor SEO opportunity
4-6: Moderate SEO opportunity
7-10: Strong SEO opportunity

## Notes to Capture

- What is missing?
- What would improve local visibility?
- Would a better landing page help?
- Does the business have strong visual/demo potential?
