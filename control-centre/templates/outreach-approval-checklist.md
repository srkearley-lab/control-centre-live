# Outreach Approval Checklist

Before sending any email, check:

- Business is relevant
- Email address is public/business-facing
- Business is not marked Do Not Contact
- Message is personalised
- Message is short and not pushy
- No false claims are made
- Demo is clearly positioned as an example/concept
- Opt-out line is included
- Shane has approved the message

## Approval Status

- Drafted
- Approved
- Sent
- Replied
- Follow-up needed
- Not interested
- Do Not Contact
