# Demo Website Brief

- Homepage goal: Help Brentwood customers quickly understand the barber services, opening hours, and how to call or visit.
- Hero message: Traditional barbering in Brentwood with experienced hands.
- Suggested sections: Hero, services, product/experience highlights, review placeholder, visit/call CTA.

- Visual style: Clean, masculine, understated, with strong contrast and simple service cards.
