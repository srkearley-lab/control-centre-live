# Website Audit

- Website status: No dedicated website found; business appears to rely on directory/listing pages.
- Design issues: Directory listing does not give the cafe a branded homepage, menu-led experience, strong photography, or clear local positioning.
- Mobile issues: Listing may be mobile-accessible, but the business does not control the journey or presentation.
- CTA issues: Phone number is visible, but there is no dedicated enquiry, menu, order, booking, or map-led call to action owned by the business.
- Trust signal issues: Reviews exist on listing pages, but there is no controlled homepage with testimonials, food photos, local sourcing, opening hours, and service highlights together.
- Overall website score: 38/100
- Why Shane should reach out: A simple mobile-first homepage could make breakfast/lunch searches convert better, show opening hours clearly, and give locals one clean place to call, find directions, and view the menu.
