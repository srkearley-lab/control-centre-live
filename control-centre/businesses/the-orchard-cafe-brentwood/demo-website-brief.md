# Demo Website Brief

- Homepage goal: Give local customers a quick, friendly way to see what the cafe offers, confirm opening hours, and call or get directions.
- Hero message: Fresh breakfast and lunch in Brentwood.
- Suggested sections: Hero, popular food, why locals visit, review placeholder, opening hours, call and directions CTA.
- Call to action: Call Now
- Visual style: Warm, clean, light, food-led, with clear typography and simple mobile spacing.
- Why the homepage would help: It would replace scattered directory information with one owned page that can rank locally and make the contact journey easier.
