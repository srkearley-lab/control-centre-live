# Demo Website Brief

- Homepage goal: Convert local dog owners into grooming enquiries with a trust-first, mobile-friendly page.
- Hero message: Gentle, professional dog grooming in Brentwood.
- Suggested sections: Hero, grooming services, why choose us, review placeholder, care promise, booking CTA.
- Call to action: Book a Groom
- Visual style: Bright, friendly, reassuring, clean cards, calm colours.
- Why the homepage would help: Pet owners need confidence quickly. A dedicated page can show care, reviews, location, and booking details without making users hunt through listings.
